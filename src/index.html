Feature Visualization with
Generative Prior Lucid

Each image pair corresponds to a channel of an extended InceptionV1 network's 5c_Branch_3_b_1x1 layer.
The network was trained on the CelebA classification task.
Left images are maximizing the mean activation among StyleGAN2 outputs.
Right images are maximizing the given channel's mean activation in pixel space.
